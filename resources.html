Pet Care Guidelines

Feeding Campus Animals

Only designated members of the Pet Committee are currently allowed to feed campus pets. Please do not feed the animals, especially around eating areas. They are fed on a regular schedule.

Interacting with Strays

Approach animals calmly. Avoid sudden movements or loud noises. If an animal appears scared or aggressive, keep a safe distance and contact the committee.
