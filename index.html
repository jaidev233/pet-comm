About Our Committee

The IIITD Pet Committee is a student-run body dedicated to ensuring the well-being of all the animals residing on our campus. We believe that every animal deserves to be treated with kindness, respect, and compassion. Our campus is home to many dogs and cats, and we consider them an integral part of the IIITD community.

Our mission is to create a safe and nurturing environment for our campus companions. We manage feeding schedules, provide medical care, conduct vaccination and sterilization drives, and run adoption programs to find loving homes for our animals.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We advocate for the humane treatment of all animals and promote empathy throughout the IIITD community.

Responsibility

We take collective responsibility for the health and safety of our campus pets, ensuring they are fed, vaccinated, and cared for.

Community

We strive to foster a harmonious coexistence between humans and animals on campus through education and engagement.
